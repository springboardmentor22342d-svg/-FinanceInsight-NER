MANAGEMENT DISCUSSION AND ANALYSIS (MD&A)
Apple Inc. reported strong financial performance in fiscal year 2023.
Total revenue reached $383 billion driven by iPhone, Mac, iPad, and Services.
Services revenue increased significantly due to App Store, iCloud, and Apple Music.
Operating income improved due to cost optimization and strong product demand.

RISK FACTORS
Apple faces intense competition in the global smartphone and technology markets.
Risks include supply chain disruptions, foreign exchange fluctuations,
cybersecurity threats, and changes in government regulations.
